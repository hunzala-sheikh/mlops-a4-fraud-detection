=== A4 Task 8 — Intelligent Retraining Strategy ===

5 sliding windows of 10k rows each. Strategies:

| strategy    | retrains | mean_auc | std_auc | cost_credits | compute_s |
|-------------|----------|----------|---------|--------------|-----------|
| threshold   |    1     |  0.8137  | 0.0154  |       5      |   0.3     |
| periodic    |    4     |  0.8140  | 0.0207  |      20      |   0.9     |
| hybrid      |    3     |  0.8175  | 0.0189  |      15      |   0.7     |

Take-away:
  - Threshold-only retraining was 4x cheaper than periodic, with 0.0003
    AUC penalty - the most efficient on this stable dataset.
  - Hybrid achieved highest mean AUC (0.8175) with only 3 retrains,
    catching one drift event the pure-threshold strategy missed.
  - Recommendation: hybrid (periodic + threshold) for production - balances
    cost-efficiency with safety net against missed drift.
