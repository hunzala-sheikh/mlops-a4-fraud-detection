How the four stages chain together
  ci.yml → build.yml → cd.yml ← intelligent_trigger.yml
  CI runs on every push, gated by ruff + pytest + schema.
  build.yml only runs after CI green; pushes both images to GHCR.
  cd.yml submits a KFP run, blocks on AUC >= 0.85, then bumps KServe.
  intelligent_trigger.yml is woken by Prometheus alerts (Task 6) and
  dispatches cd.yml with the latest data window.
