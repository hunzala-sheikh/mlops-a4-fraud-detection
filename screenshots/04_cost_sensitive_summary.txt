Business take-away: with fraud_cost / fp_cost = 20:1, the standard model
already minimises total cost. cost_sens_x3 reduces fraud loss by $10k but
incurs $15.8k more in false-alarm costs => net loss $5.8k. Higher
penalties only help if fp_cost falls below ~$30/case (e.g., automated
review pipelines vs human reviewers).
